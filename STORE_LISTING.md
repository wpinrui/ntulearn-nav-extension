## Short Description

> A smarter NTULearn experience — course switcher, folder expansion, download buttons, dark theme, and more.

## Detailed Description

NTUitive makes NTULearn (Blackboard Ultra) faster and easier to use.

COURSE SWITCHER
The built-in "Recent courses" dropdown only shows a handful of courses. NTUitive replaces it with a searchable list of every course you're enrolled in. Just start typing to find what you need.

COURSE LINKS FIX
Course cards on the course list page don't behave like normal links — ctrl+click and right-click "Open in new tab" don't work. NTUitive fixes this so you can open courses however you like.

AUTO-EXPAND FOLDERS
Tired of clicking through nested folders? NTUitive automatically expands folders in the course outline so you can see all your content at a glance. You can configure how many levels deep it goes.

PDF VIEWER FULLSCREEN
Document previews are cramped by default. NTUitive expands them to fill your screen.

SURFACE DOWNLOAD BUTTON
The download option is buried inside a "..." menu. NTUitive puts it right on the page where you can see it.

All features can be toggled individually from the extension popup. A few additional experimental features (dark theme, back button fix) are also available and can be enabled in settings.

The course switcher needs you to visit the course list page once so it can learn your courses.

Privacy: NTUitive does not collect or transmit any data. Everything stays in your browser.
